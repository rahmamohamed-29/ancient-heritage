Grand Egyptian Museum

A massive museum near the Giza Pyramids. It displays key ancient Egyptian artifacts, including Tutankhamun’s collection. A modern take on Egypt’s history.

Egyptian Museum

Home to a vast collection of ancient Egyptian treasures in Cairo. Famous for Tutankhamun’s artifacts. A true reflection of Egypt’s ancient heritage.

Museum of Islamic Art

Houses one of the finest collections of Islamic artifacts. Features rare pieces from different eras and regions. Showcases the beauty of Islamic culture.

Coptic Museum

Displays Egypt’s Christian heritage through unique artifacts. Includes manuscripts, icons, and textiles. Reflects Coptic art and traditions.

National Museum of Egyptian Civilization

Covers Egypt’s history from ancient to modern times. Known for the Royal Mummies Hall. Offers a complete journey through Egyptian civilization.
